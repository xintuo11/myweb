Tangxian Xintuo Import And Export Trading Co., Ltd.

Enable everyone to have affordable art sculptures

Tangxian Xintuo Import and Export Trading Co., Ltd., also named Tangxian Baocheng Sculpture Crafts Manufacturing Co., Ltd., is located in Tangxian, the hometown of bronze sculpture. We have over 20 years of experience in producing and making sculptures. So far, there are tens of thousands of sculpture styles for you to choose from, meeting your decoration needs in different spaces. We also have a professional 3D design and clay sculpture production team, which can create 1:1 sculptures based on the pictures or CAD provided by you. Throughout the entire design and production process, we will follow up on the progress of each step, coordinate your needs in a timely manner, and ultimately achieve your satisfactory results. Established in April 2014, the company has a professional team of designers, molds, casting, welding, polishing, and painting, as well as excellent designers and artists. Through unremitting efforts, our products have evolved from a single bronze sculpture to a variety of material sculptures and handicrafts. And established an international trade department in 2010 to undertake international order business.
